PRIVACY STATEMENT

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • name
  • contact information
  • how you interactions with us.

We collect your personal information in order to:

  • keep in contact with you and inform you the information about your reservations for dining
  • use it for gathering analytical information about how the website is used to allow us to improve the user experience.

Besides our staff, we may share this information with:

  • an analytics vender in order to generate analytics data as a part of marketing process for the restaurant.

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter your name and contact information, we'll be unable to contact you or inform you any information about your reservations.

Also, if you choose not to enable the ability to store cookies in your web browser, we'll be unable to use analytics to improve the overall visitor experience.

We keep your information safe by storing it in encrypted database and only allowing admin or staff to access it.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you'd like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at admin@haukai.co.nz, or 09 401 4019, or 1 Island Street, Kerikeri, Bay of Islands.
