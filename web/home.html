DELICIOUS, AUTHENTIC MĀORI KAI IN THE BAY OF ISLANDS

Our bistro-style restaurant specializes in Māori kai and is based in the Bay of Islands. Proudly being set amongst the vines, you can sit on the deck and enjoy the relaxed vibe. Fresh local produce is paramount to producing dishes that are honest, wholesome, and full of flavour.

Lunch and dinner menus adapt to the flavours of the seasons and are constantly evolving whilst maintaining a common thread.

Dining at the Haukai is an excellent way to sample the best that the Bay of Islands has to offer, whilst being perfectly complemented by our wide-ranged wines.
